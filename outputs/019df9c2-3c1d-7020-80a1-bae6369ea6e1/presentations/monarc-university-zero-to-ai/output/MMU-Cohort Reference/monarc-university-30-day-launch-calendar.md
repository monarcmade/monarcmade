## Daily Short Formula

Use this structure for Shorts/Reels/TikToks:

1. Hook: "If I was learning AI from zero..."
2. One idea: explain one step or mistake
3. Concrete example: business, content, client, or career
4. CTA: "I am building this into Monarc University. Apply if you want the guided version."

## Lead Scoring Notes

High-intent applicants usually mention:

- A specific business process
- A client/service idea
- A project they want to build
- A budget range above $1,500
- Interest in VIP/build-with-me
- A timeline or urgency

Low-intent applicants usually mention:

- "Just curious"
- No clear project
- No reason for learning AI
- Only wants free content
- No time commitment
