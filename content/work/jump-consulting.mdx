## Context

Jump Consulting needed a **serious editorial home**: courses, podcasts, and offers all competing for attention without turning the site into a maze. Design arrived as **Figma**; the mandate was WordPress with **room to grow**.

## What Monarc Made focused on

- **IA and templates** — page types, taxonomies, and reusable blocks that matched the content model.
- **Figma → WordPress** — spacing, typography, and component behavior that survived real copy lengths.
- **Editor experience** — guardrails so marketing could publish without calling engineering for every tweak.

## How it landed

The site shipped as a **coherent system**: editors could add programs and media without breaking layout, SEO-critical structure stayed consistent, and the build matched the brand as designed—not a watered-down theme approximation.
