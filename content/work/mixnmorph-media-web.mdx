## Context

Mix N Morph Media needed a site that **sold creative work** while staying fast to update: new reels, new clients, and shifting offers. The build had to be **honest about SEO**—structure and semantics—not keyword stuffing.

## What Monarc Made focused on

- **Template and section model** — repeatable patterns for case highlights, services, and team story.
- **Performance and clarity** — pages that stayed readable on mobile where prospects actually browse portfolios.
- **Editorial workflow** — WordPress patterns that matched how the agency writes and publishes.

## How it landed

The agency got a **flexible marketing surface**: content could rotate with campaigns, technical SEO stayed in the bones of the templates, and the team could ship updates without treating the site as a fragile art piece.
