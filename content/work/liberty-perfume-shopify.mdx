## Context

Liberty Perfume needed a **full storefront refresh** on Shopify: structure felt dated, product imagery and typography were inconsistent, and the path from discovery to cart did not match how shoppers actually move through fragrance categories.

## What Monarc Made focused on

- **Shopify + Liquid** — theme architecture, sections, and templates that stay maintainable as the catalog grows.
- **Site structure and IA** — collections, featured products, and supporting pages wired for clarity and SEO-friendly URLs.
- **Visual system and UX** — imagery, spacing, and interaction patterns tuned for a cleaner first impression and fewer dead ends at checkout.

## How it landed

The store ships as a **cohesive Shopify surface**: structure supports merchandising experiments, the visual system reads consistently across devices, and the implementation stays inside native Shopify patterns so day-to-day updates stay in the merchant workflow.
